Gray Quarter - Implementation Consultant

Job Description

Gray Quarter is looking for a talented Implementation Consultant for our Accela implementation and managed service practice. In this exciting role you will work with multiple City, County, and State Governments to design and build solutions for customers using the Accela Civic Platform.


Responsibilities
  • Lead analysis sessions and capture business requirements for a variety of domains including Land Management, Business Licensing, Professional Licensing, Code Enforcement, Asset Management.
  • Design solutions that meet customer needs
  • Develop, test, and document completed solutions
  • Provide end user and technical training on completed solutions
  • Participate in sales opportunities
  • Work with the Gray Quarter team to expand our out of the box solution offerings
  • Look for opportunities to expand the Gray Quarter iPaaS offerings
  • Participate in other technical components (Scripting, Data Conversion/Migration, Report Development)
Requirements
  • 5+ years of consulting implementation experience with Commercial off the Shelf (COTS) software
  • 1+ years of implementing the Accela Civic Platform
  • Bachelors Degree in Information Technology or related field
  • Strong written and verbal communication
  • Strong presentation experience
  • Experience with both Agile and Waterfall methodologies
  • JavaScript experience
  • Ability to travel 50-75%
  • Ability to work from a home office
  • Passion for working with government
Pluses
  • SQL Script experience
  • Report Development using SSRS or Crystal Reports
  • C# .NET, Java, or Nodejs programming
  • Project Management and/or Experience leading Managed Services accounts
*No Recruiters or Agencies Please
